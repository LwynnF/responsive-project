Then.

Prior to my coding and SheCodes journey, I was working in the finance sector and after three years, I knew it was time for a new challenge. I have always had an interest in tech and loved problem solving from a young age, whether this be in the form of sudokus or playing Professor Layton on my Nintendo DS.

One day on Instagram, I started to notice ads for SheCodes and I decided to join the SheCodes Express live coding class. It amazed me how quickly I could see the result of the code and that coding is not as scary as it seems. After that class, I signed myself up for the SheCodes Basics course and the rest is history!

Now.

After completing the SheCodes Basics course, I felt that I had finally found a new passion and loved the satisfaction from solving coding errors. As of right now, I am currently participating in the SheCodes Pro workshop - creating this portfolio as part of my SheCodes Responsive final project.

I find that coding is best learnt through practice and by participating in the SheCodes challenges I am able to put my newly acquired skills and knowledge into practice and have fun along the way.

Next.

Upon completion of the workshops and having upskilled - learning CSS, HTML, Javascript, React and more technologies, I hope I am equipped with the skills to break into the tech industry.

I chose to transition from finance to tech as I love the freedom for creativity, the rewarding satisfaction from cracking coding issues but most importantly I wanted to pursue a new career where typically women are underpresented and to help bridge the gender gap in tech.
